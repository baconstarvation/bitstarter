Bloksy - Discover and Publish

Bloksy helps you read, consume, and post interesting content on-the-go in a way that is easily accessible.

Mobile users want a frictionless way to post, schedule, and save interesting articles that they find on their blogs and social media channels - including Twitter, Facebook, Tumblr, Wordpress, and Squarespace. They also want to read and consume beautiful content on-the-go in a way that is easily accessible. Ease of functionality should encourage both sophisticated and casual users to post content to their networks more frequently. Users will be able to discover and post content without ever having to leave the application.

This is what the people want to do. This is what I hope to give you: Easily schedule and post content (articles, video, and pictures) on blog and social media channels. Use a tool with an easy learning curve. Sophisticated Users: Schedule and publish on the fly. Sophisticated Users: Content archival - keep a readable record of things posted on social media channels and blogs. Casual Users: Post interesting articles and content to blog and social media channels once or twice a week. Casual Users: Share interesting things with friends for fun. All Users: Enjoy a cohesive experience.
